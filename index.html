About Me

I am a creative and passionate individual currently in 10th grade with a strong love for art and traveling. I enjoy expressing myself through creativity and exploring new places, cultures, and experiences. In the future, I hope to become a fashion designer and model, combining my artistic vision with my interest in style and self-expression. My goal is to travel the world as often as possible, gaining inspiration from different environments and people along the way.



My Favorite Albums

My music taste reflects a deep appreciation for creativity, storytelling, and emotion. Albums like Blond, Camp, Grace, Freudian, Charm, and Older (and Wiser) each bring something unique, from introspective lyrics to smooth melodies and bold artistic expression. I enjoy how these projects explore themes like identity, love, growth, and self-discovery, often blending different genres and sounds. Together, they represent my interest in music that feels personal, meaningful, and creatively inspiring.



Destinations

Traveling, for me, isn’t just about going to new places. It’s about experiencing different cultures, seeing new things, and learning more about myself along the way. Visiting cities like London and Paris or relaxing in the Caribbean has shown me how big and diverse the world really is. Even trips I didn’t get to take, like Hawaii, Egypt, or Dubai, make me excited to see more someday. Travel is more than vacations it’s about growing, exploring, and making memories that stay with you.
